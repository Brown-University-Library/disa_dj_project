Between 2.5 and 5 million Natives were enslaved in the Americas between 1492 and 1900.

This site begins to tell their stories.

Find out more

Stolen Relations is a community-based project that seeks to understand and illuminate the significance of the enslavement of Indigenous peoples in settler colonialism through the recovery of individual stories.

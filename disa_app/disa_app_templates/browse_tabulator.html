A note on terminology: information about individuals are recorded from "items" (such as an advertisement announcing a person fleeing enslavement) that are contained in "documents" (such as a newspaper).

Experimental Search

Welcome, community collaborators! This is a preliminary, experimental search portal of the Database of Indigenous Slavery in the Americas, which allows you to search the approximately 3,000 individuals drawn from archival records regarding the enslavement of indigenous populations in the Americas, from the 17th to the 19th centuries.

As you explore, please understand that this database is under development and may contain errors, and will continuously be updated.

We welcome any and all feedback, corrections, and suggestions for future development — please contact linford_fisher@brown.edu.
